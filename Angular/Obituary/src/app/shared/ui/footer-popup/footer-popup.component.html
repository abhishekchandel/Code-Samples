What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files made up of letters and numbers stored in the web browser directories on visitors’ computers or internet-enabled devices. Generally, cookies may contain information about the website visitor, his internet protocol (“IP”) addresses, the region or general location of the device used to access the website, browser type, operating system, the visitor’s preferences, and his use of the EveryObit.com website, including a history of the pages viewed. While the information in a cookie does not generally identify the specific website visitor, it can provide visitors with a more personalized user experience. To find out more about cookies and how to manage and delete them, visit http://www.allaboutcookies.org/ or https://www.aboutcookies.org/.

EveryObit.com uses the following types of cookies for its website:

  • Essential Cookies: These are cookies that we need to use for our website to function and cannot be turned off in our system, and are usually set in response to some action you have taken equivalent to you requesting a service, e.g., setting privacy preferences or logging into your client portal. If you decide to block or get alerts about these cookies, some parts of our website may not work. Some of these cookies, such as the ones associated with logging into your client portal, store personally identifiable information.

  • Non-essential Cookies: Performance, visits and traffic source, and analytics cookies (e.g., Google Analytics). These are cookies that we use to count visits and sources of traffic to our website so that we can measure and improve its performance and decide which pages are the most or least popular. The information these cookies collect is aggregated and anonymous. This website uses Google Analytics cookies. Information collected by these cookies will be transferred to and stored by Google in accordance with its privacy practices. For further information on Google’s privacy practices and how this applies to Google Analytics, please visit https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/6004245.
